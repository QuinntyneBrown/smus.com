About the author

Hey! I'm most passionate about creating useful, enjoyable user interfaces (in the broadest sense of the word), especially at the cusp of hardware and software. In my projects, I tend to gravitate to web technologies because of the flexibility of the platform.

I'm currently employed as a software engineer working on emerging user interfaces at Google Research. Prior to that I was on the Chrome developer relations team, doing my part to bring more awesomeness to the modern web platform.

Before Google, I was a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University, where I focused on wearable and social computing research. Prior to that, I worked at Apple for two years, contributing to iWeb, iWork and iWork.com.
